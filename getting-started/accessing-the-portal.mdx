The Redwood Cloud Portal is available at [portal.redwood.cloud](https://portal.redwood.cloud/). Access is credential-based — your Redwood account team provides your initial login details when your organization is onboarded. Once signed in, you can connect to your product environments, manage users, and perform administrative tasks across your Redwood SaaS products.

## Supported browsers

Use a current, fully updated version of any of the following browsers:

- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Apple Safari

Older browser versions and Internet Explorer are not supported. If you encounter display or functionality issues, updating your browser resolves most problems.

## Sign in to the portal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the portal">
    Navigate to [portal.redwood.cloud](https://portal.redwood.cloud/) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter the email address and password provided by your Redwood account team, then select **Sign In**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete any required verification">
    If your organization has multi-factor authentication (MFA) configured, you will be prompted to verify your identity. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept terms on first login">
    On your first login, you may be asked to review and accept the terms of use. Read and accept to proceed to the portal home.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your credentials are specific to your organization. If you have multiple organizations or contracts with Redwood, your account team will advise you on how access is structured.
</Note>

## First-time login experience

When you sign in for the first time, the portal displays your organization's available environments and the main navigation areas. Take a moment to review the **Environments** section, which lists your active Redwood SaaS product instances (RunMyJobs, Finance Automation, JSCAPE).

If no environments appear, your provisioning may still be in progress. Contact your Redwood account team or reach out to [docsupport@redwood.com](mailto:docsupport@redwood.com) for assistance.

<Tip>
  Bookmark [portal.redwood.cloud](https://portal.redwood.cloud/) for quick access. The portal does not require a VPN and is accessible from any supported browser with an internet connection.
</Tip>

## Reset your password

If you forget your password or cannot sign in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select 'Forgot password'">
    On the sign-in page, select the **Forgot password?** link below the sign-in form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Enter the email address associated with your portal account and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    You will receive a password reset link. Open the email and follow the instructions to set a new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your new password">
    Return to [portal.redwood.cloud](https://portal.redwood.cloud/) and sign in using your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Password reset links expire after a limited time. If your link has expired, repeat the reset process to receive a new one.
</Warning>

## Troubleshoot login issues

| Issue | What to do |
|-------|-----------|
| You did not receive a password reset email | Check your spam or junk folder. If the email is not there, confirm you entered the correct email address. |
| Your account is locked | Contact your organization's portal administrator or email [docsupport@redwood.com](mailto:docsupport@redwood.com). |
| You receive an "unauthorized" or "access denied" error | Your account may not have been provisioned yet. Contact your Redwood account team. |
| The portal does not load | Confirm your browser is supported and up to date. Try clearing your browser cache or opening the portal in a private/incognito window. |

## Get help

If you have questions about accessing the portal or need your account set up, contact the Redwood support team at [docsupport@redwood.com](mailto:docsupport@redwood.com).
