## What you can do in the portal

The Redwood Cloud Portal gives you full control over your Redwood SaaS products:

- **Connect to environments** — Access your RunMyJobs, Finance Automation, and JSCAPE instances directly from the portal.
- **Manage environments** — View environment status, configuration, and health at a glance.
- **Schedule upgrades** — Plan and manage product upgrades with configurable upgrade windows to minimize disruption.
- **Administer users** — Create and manage user accounts and assign roles with appropriate permissions.
- **Configure SSO** — Set up Single Sign-On to streamline access for your organization.
- **Monitor activity** — Track all user and automated actions with detailed audit logs.
- **Manage notifications** — Configure and review alerts for your environments.
- **View consumption** — Access contract details and track product usage and consumption.

<Note>
  To access the Redwood Cloud Portal, visit [portal.redwood.cloud](https://portal.redwood.cloud/). You will need credentials provided by your Redwood account team.
</Note>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the portal">
    Navigate to [portal.redwood.cloud](https://portal.redwood.cloud/) and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to your environments">
    From the portal home, locate your product environments and connect to RunMyJobs, Finance Automation, or JSCAPE.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your team">
    Create user accounts and assign roles so your team members can access the environments they need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure upgrades">
    Set upgrade windows that align with your maintenance schedule to control when product updates are applied.
  </Step>
</Steps>
